Protective tips
😷
Wear N95 masks
Use certified N95 respirators when AQI exceeds 100 for proper protection against fine particles.
🪟
Seal windows
Keep windows and doors closed on high AQI days. Run your HVAC in recirculation mode.
🌿
Use air purifiers
HEPA air purifiers can reduce indoor particle concentration by up to 95%.
🏃
Time your exercise
Exercise outdoors in the early morning when AQI is typically at its daily lowest.
